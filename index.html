Numericle

How to Play
Welcome to Numericle!! You have to guess a number(can have leading zeroes).
Choose your Level:
  • EASY: Guess a 6 digit number in 7 tries
  • MEDIUM: Guess a 7 digit number in 7 tries
  • EASY: Guess a 8 digit number in 8 tries
Green: The digit entered is at the correct position as the answer.
Yellow: The digit entered is present in the answer but not at the given position.
Grey: The digit entered is not present in the answer.

KEYS:
