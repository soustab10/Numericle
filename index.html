Numericle

How to Play
Welcome to Numericle!! You have to guess a number with 6 digits(can have leading zeroes) within 7 tries.
Green: The digit entered is at the correct position as the answer.
Yellow: The digit entered is present in the answer but not at the given position.
Grey: The digit entered is not present in the answer.

KEYS:
ENTER: To submit your guess. You can only submit a 6 digit number as guess.
<<: Backspace Key
